Hike Update: Aug 2017

September 13, 2017 by Hiren Savjiyani

What’s New in Hike? August releases

It’s been a busy year for Hike, with more and more retailers choosing us as their preferred Point of Sale. Since we

introduced Hike 3.0 in May, we’ve listened to all your feedback and are now excited to share with you the newest improvements in Hike.

New features

View reports by outlet

We’ve made it easier for you to switch between locations if you’re using Hike for more than one outlet. A new dropdown box will enable you to switch between locations to view the latest reports and analytics for each.

You can also set staff permissions whereby a manager for one store only has access to that store. This manager won’t be able to access the cash register for any other store.

Locking products during stocktake

Based on feedback from you, we’ve implemented product locking, so that you’ll never sell a product that under stocktake. From now on, when you lock a product, you or your staff will not be able to sell that product until the counting has been completed.

If someone tries to sell a locked product, Hike will put up a Locked Error Message and he/she will be unable to add that product to the shopping cart.

Add product into product type from product type page

Save valuable time by being able to create products directly in the product type page. Add a product type and link associated products by whatever category you need.

Add custom data fields to products

You can now enter in two new fields when creating a product: Supplier Code & Custom Field.

Track your products via supplier and/or label your products through your own in-house reference codes via the custom field.

Enhanced card security

To increase security, only administrators of Hike POS will be able to view credit and gift card numbers. The last 3 digits on card numbers will be hidden, to protect customers from theft and fraud.

Sales history filter introduction

Filter your sales history by customer, register, sale reference number (#), or barcode to search and select individual transactions.

Connect multiple outlets to multiple 3rd party applications

If you own multiple outlets in different locations, you can now have each of those stores connected to multiple eCommerce sites and accounting platforms. Perfect for franchise owners that want to be able to easily distinguish the reporting of each individual store, to find out which one is performing best.

Chinese traditional

Hike now supports Traditional Chinese as a language for its POS solution.

Company name

You can now add the company name of a customer into the customer list display.

Buy one get free

In giving users (and customers) a more robust discount offering, Hike now gives you an option to set buy one get one free discount codes for your products. Set the parameters to how you see fit and reward your customers.

Printing & Receipts

Jewellery labels

Print out jewellery labels for your smaller items, that can be attached to your products. Make it easier for your staff by printing these labels, so that they can scan the barcodes instead of having to search for the product when making a sale.

Any size logo

Increase or reduce the size of your store’s logo, as they appear on your receipts and invoices.

Responsive template

Hike now offers a responsive template, which means if you’re printing invoices on A4 paper, Hike will now readjust its printing to ensure that it appears in true A4 format. The font size and spacing will all be designed to fit the paper size.

Display line item discount on receipt

If any of your products are being sold with discounts, you now have the option to show or hide the discount total on your receipts.

Cash in Cash out notes

Any notes that you place on your CICO withdrawals/deposits will now appear in your end of day, cash register invoice. Know the reasons behind why petty cash is being taken in and out of your business.

Show tax inclusive price

You can now change your printer label settings to show prices inclusive of tax prices or exclusive. This can be done in the General Settings of Hike.

User interface

Add notes on parked orders

You can now enter notes on parked orders such as the customer’s name or an order reference number. This makes it easier for you to find that parked order, once the sale transaction is finalised.

Lazy scrolling on enter sale page

Instead of clicking on multiple pages to view your products in the sales screen, you’ll now be able to infinitely scroll down on the one page to select your items. View your entire product catalogue in a faster, more streamlined way.

New chat support icon

Our new support icon makes it easier for you to find the help you want. Whether you want to chat with one of our live support technicians, visit our extensive support guides or watch video tutorials on Hike, you can now do so with just a click of the button, anytime.

FadeIn/FadeOut

When adding a product to your cart, new FadeIn & FadeOut animations will appear, making it easier for your cashier to know when a product has been added or removed.

Permission pop-up in user UI to show as single tree

We now have an improved user interface for managing user permissions. Pop-ups in the UI are now reflected as a single tree.

Performance

Speed improvement on sales history page

Hike’s performance upgrades have made viewing your sales history, a much faster process.

Bulk import to improve speed in import and PO

Bulk importing now takes drastically less time, when bringing across copious amounts of data.

Report performance improvement

Viewing your real-time retail reports will take no time at all, as Hike has been streamlined exponentially to give you the fastest POS.

Upgraded visual fidelity of product images

Not only will your products look better on Hike but your product images will now also appear in your cart as well.
