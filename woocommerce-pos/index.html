WooCommerce POS

Use Hike as your WooCommerce POS to empower your business with a robust retail solution.

POS for WooCommerce on WordPress

WooCommerce on WordPress is a great way to sell online. Hike is a brilliant in-store point of sale solution. Hike seamlessly integrates with WooCommerce, giving you central access to all your customers, inventory, product catalog and more.

Control and manage every aspect of your business, from an all-in-one retail POS solution.

Hike + WooCommerce features list

Sync existing catalog

Already using Hike or WooCommerce? Automatically sync your existing inventory from Hike to Woo or other way around.

Seamless inventory

Sales, purchases, inventory take or any other transaction will instantly update inventor on both platforms: Hike and WooCommerce.

Select sync option

Select the Hike inventory you want to appear on your WooCommerce store, whether that’s your entire range of products or select products from your outlets.

Sync orders

Any orders placed in WooCommerce will be immediately synced to Hike. Your staff can manage both in-store and online orders using Hike’s sales dashboard.

Customer profiles

Existing user profile updates and new user accounts are sync’d from Hike to Woo and vice-a-versa.

Analytics

With complete sales integration, use Hike’s sales reporting to gain insights into both in-store and online sales.

Robust product management

A centralized inventory and catalog means no more double entry. Easily add, update or remove an item using Hike and changes are instantly reflected in your WooCommerce store.

Get started in minutes

Getting started with WooCommerce POS integration is easy. Simply link your online store with Hike, customize your settings and that’s it!

Detailed sync log

Hike keeps accessible data sync log. If something fails to sync, simply check the log to see what failed and why. The log lets you zero in on the information that you require to fix broken data.

Absolutely free

WooCommerce POS integration is free and available to all Hike retailers with active subscription.
