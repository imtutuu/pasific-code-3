Hike + Westpac

Helping you save time with fast and seamless payments, connected directly to your point of sale.

 

Hike is proud to partner with Westpac’s new integrated payments solution, Presto Smart

Designed to make in-store payments fast by directly connecting your POS to your Presto Smart payment terminal — taking away the hassle of manually keying in transactions and speeding up sales at check out.

Presto Smart is a compact and lightweight EFTPOS terminal, letting you take payments anywhere in-store – on the counter or on the sales floor, giving you back time to do what you do best, running your business.

iPad, PC or Mac

Use it with Hike cloud version on PC & Mac. Also works with Hike register iPad app.

Accept all card payments

Accept all debit and credit cards with swipe, insert & tap & go payment options.

Fast & reliable

Sale amounts are automatically posted to Westpac terminals for faster, error free transactions.

Payments on-the-go

Let your customers securely pay at the counter or bring your payment terminals to where they are!

Benefits of using Westpac Presto Smart

Fast payments & simple reconciliation

No more manual typing of payment amounts into your merchant terminal, minimising keying errors and making end-of-day reconciliation fuss-free.

Stable & reliable

On the VX690 terminal, Presto Smart will automatically switch over to 3G should your main communication method drop out so you never miss a sale.

24/7 local support

You’re covered with 24/7, local technical support on 1800 029 749.

Instant settlement

Immediate access to your funds 365 days a year*
