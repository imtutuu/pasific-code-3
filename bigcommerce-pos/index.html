Sync your BigCommerce Online Store

Hike features a native integration with BigCommerce, the leading eCommerce platform used by more than 50,000+ online stores. By integrating BigCommerce and Hike, you’re getting the most innovative solution to selling both in-store and online.

Why use Hike as your BigCommerce POS

Get started in minutes

2-way sync between Hike & BigCommerce allows you to start selling in minutes. If you’re already selling on BigCommerce, simply sync your catalogue and all your products are ready to sell in-store.

Inventory sync

Link your brick and mortar store/warehouse inventory with BigCommerce. All in-store & online sales will automatically update inventory on both platforms.

Central management

Increase efficiency with central management. Update any of your customer, inventory or product details in either system and it will instantly reflect everywhere.

Order sync

Orders placed in your BigCommerce store are instantly sync’d to Hike so you can offer in-store pick-up, exchange and even allow customers to pay in store on pickup.

Multi-store POS

Hike is a multi-store POS & retail management solution. Add more locations at anytime with a click of a button. It grows with your business.

Real-time analytics

Get all in-store & online sales analytics in one platform and that too in real-time. It makes financial and analytical reporting easier than ever.

Hike POS + BigCommerce features

Inventory sync

All your sales, inventory count and new inventory updates are automatically sync’d between Hike & BigCommerce. You can specify which store’s inventory you wish to display online on the BigCommerce.

Select products sync

Choose which products you wish to sync from Hike to BigCommerce. You can sync everything you have or simply uncheck the products that you do not want to sell online.

Auto import/export

Activate your Hike + BigCommerce integration, specify your settings and Hike will instantly sync your products to/from Hike. All future updates and inventory are instantly sync’d to BigCommerce.

Order sync

Online orders received in your BigCommerce store are instantly sync’d to Hike. Your entire sales ledger, reports and analytics are centralized.

Central product management

Manage a centralized product catalog in Hike. All updates are instantly sync’d with BigCommerce. Got products that you sell only in BigCommerce? Not a problem. Simply deactivate in-store sales at the click of a button.

Central customer profiles

With new orders from BigCommerce, Hike brings all the relevant data including customer profiles. Customer profile changes and updates are streamlined across all outlets and online store.

Reports & analytics

Use Hike’s reporting dashboard to report on both in-store and online sales. View and understand consolidated and stand alone sales numbers for your in-store and BigCommerce sales channels.

Log reports

Access comprehensive log reports on data sync’d to and from Hike including details on anything that failed to sync along with its reason. Quickly Zero in on the information that you require.

Real-time sync

All inventory updates and changes are instantly sent to BigCommerce. Similarly, updates received from BigCommerce are instantly reflected in Hike.

Absolutely free for Hike users

Hike + BigCommerce integration is developed and managed by Hike team and is available to all Hike users at no additional cost.
