Use Hike as your Shopify POS

Instantly start selling your Shopify products in-store with Hike as Shopify POS for your retail outlets.

Why use Hike as your Shopify POS

Get started in minutes

2-way sync between Hike & Shopify allows you to start selling in minutes. If you’re already selling on Shopify, simply sync your catalog and all your products are ready to sell in-store.

Inventory sync

Link your brick-and-martar store/warehouse inventory with Shopify. All in-store & online sales will automatically update inventory on both platforms.

Central management

Increase efficiency with central management. Update any of your customer, inventory or product details in either system and it will instantly reflect everywhere.

Order sync

Orders placed in your Shopify store are instantly sync’d to Hike so you can offer in-store pick-up, exchange and even allow customers to pay in store on pickup.

Multi-store POS

Hike is a multi-store POS & retail management solution. Add more locations at anytime with a click of a button. It grows with your business.

Real-time analytics

Get all in-store & online sales analytics in one platform and that too in real-time. It makes financial and analytical reporting easier than ever.

Hike POS + Shopify integration features

Inventory sync

Once activated, Hike integration will take care of making your inventory centralized. Sell on Hike or Shopify, your inventory updates will automatically update on both platforms. If you sell from multiple locations, Hike lets you select which store’s inventory gets linked with your Shopify store.

Sync all or some products online? You choose.

You may choose to sell some items only in stores or online. You can sync everything you have or simply uncheck the products that you do not want to sell online.

Ready to sell in minutes

Once Hike POS and Shopify integration is activated, Hike will instantly sync your products to/from Hike. All future changes to individual product details are instantly updated.

Order sync

Online orders received in your Shopify store are instantly sync’d to Hike. This is so your entire sales ledger, reports and analytics are centralized.

Central product management

Hike lets you manage a centralized product catalogue. Whether you sell a product in-store and/or online, Hike is where you can add and update your entire catalogue.

Central customer profiles

With new orders from Shopify, Hike brings all the relevant data including customer profiles. All updates to customer profiles are streamlined across outlets and online store.

Reports & analytics

Use Hike’s reporting dashboard to report on both in-store and Shopify sales. Review consolidated and standalone sales numbers for your in-store and online sales channels.

Detailed sync log

Hike keeps detailed log on data sync’d between Hike and Shopify. If something fails to sync, simply check the log to see what and why it did not sync. The log lets you zero in on the information that you require.

Real-time sync

All sync data that’s available from Shopify in real-time are updated instantly. Helps you avoid overselling and know in advance when that popular product is running low in inventory.

Absolutely free for Hike & Shopify users

Hike + Shopify integration is 100% developed and managed by Hike team and so is available to all Hike and Shopify users at no additional cost.

Ready to get started?

If you’re already a Hike and Shopify user, simply login to Hike, go to integrations and connect Shopify. If you’re a new user get started with a free trial!
