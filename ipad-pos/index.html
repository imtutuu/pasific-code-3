iPad POS system for on-the-go sales

Pick & pack online orders with your iPad

Setting up your iPad with Hike is not a difficult process, but for someone new into retail and POS systems, it will take some time to ensure everything is running smoothly on your first day.

Generally, before you can begin selling on Hike you will need to:

  1. Set-up your General Settings and Tax Rules in Hike POS
  2. Import or create all your products, customers & suppliers into Hike POS.
  3. Connect your retail printer, barcode scanner, cash drawer, payment processor (where applicable).
