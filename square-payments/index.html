Sell more and stay in control with Hike and Square

Hike POS for Square enables businesses to streamline their payments, maximise their potential and keep customer satisfaction at the forefront of their commitments.

Square is fast, secure, payment processing solution that integrates seamlessly with Hike’s POS all-in-one retail software, providing businesses everything that they need to spend less time worrying about collecting payments and more time growing their business.

  • Fast, secure and reliable payment processing.
  • Supporting a range of payment methods.
  • Transparent pricing – no hidden fees, only pay when you make a sale.
  • Next day business transfers.
  • Free dispute management. Square does the legwork for you to sort it, even covering any bank fees that may be incurred.
  • PCI compliance covered and active fraud prevention.

Benefits of Hike’s integration with Square

Plug-n-play compatibility

Once your iPad and Square hardware is ready, just download Hike and select Square as your chosen payment processor. No bank visits or merchant accounts required. It really is that easy.

Perfect synchronicity

Manual card terminal entries leave room for human error. When you choose Hike as your Square POS integration, all of the transaction data is seamlessly and automatically synchronised with the card terminal – simple and error-free!

Simple, seamless payment processing – anywhere

Keep your customer’s satisfied with a smooth, straightforward payment experience – the whole process carried out on one easy-to-use device. Since Hike is cloud-based, payments can be taken anywhere – in person in store, over the phone and even online.

More ways to pay

Hike’s integration with Square gives customers even more ways to pay for their goods and/or services, including accepting chip cards, magstripe and NFC payments such as Apple Pay. Choose a dedicated sales desk or let your sales team take payments directly from the shop floor. Use your existing Square hardware – there’s no need to upgrade.

Comprehensive reporting

Hike’s POS software works with Square to track sales, revenue, profits, inventory and more, keeping you firmly in control of your store and able to make real-time decisions from anywhere in the world.

Easy access to your money

Every retailer wants easy to access to their money and with Hike’s Square integration, you will see the money in your account within 2 business days. Even better, you’ll pay the same rate for all major credit cards, maximizing your profits.

Unharness your business growth

Want to grow your business hassle-free? You can use your Square terminal and Hike POS to connect with a range of other popular business apps, such as Shopify, Quickbooks Online, Xero, MYOB and Unleashed.

Protect your customer’s data

Square and Hike take data protection extremely seriously, removing most of the hassle and stress from business owners. Between us we constantly monitor for suspicious activity, and should anything be spotted, you can be sure that action is taken right away.
