Omnichannel Retail Solution

Expand your audience by connecting your online & in-store sales under one sophisticated omnichannel retail solution. Grow your customer base and ‘hike-up’ sales today.

Explaining omnichannel retailing

Omnichannel retailing refers to a streamlined connection between your physical and digital channels. By incorporating a robust omnichannel retail solution, your customers can connect with your online, in-store and on-the-go shopping platforms seamlessly.

Centralized data

With your omnichannel POS solution, everything from your inventory to your sales orders and customers are connected under the one, centrally located system. So as your customers shop with freedom through all your various platforms, your entire database – products, customer information, etc. – will be updated automatically in real-time.

Expand your store with eCommerce

A big part of the omnichannel ecosystem is your online profile, especially your online store. Boost your sales capacity by incorporating a robust eCommerce site with Hike. Additionally, Hike has partnered with leading eCommerce apps like Shopify and BigCommerce, to provide retailers even more scope in how they present their online store.

Omnichannel analytics

Never lose sight of your business trends and statistics, with Hike’s advanced reporting. Retailers learn in real-time, pertinent information on their store, helping them understand the aspects of the business that are working well, and that aspects that are not. Hike’s useful analytics is designed to help you make smarter business decisions.

With the modern shopper in mind…

Shopping in today’s world is all about accessibility, which is why having an omnichannel retail solution is imperative for retailers to utilise. With the exponential rise in online shopping, having all your bases covered is a great way to attract more customers who may all shop in different ways.
